Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MeemoAI and how to use our services effectively.

Getting Started

What is MeemoAI?
MeemoAI is an advanced AI-powered platform that helps users streamline their workflow, automate tasks, and enhance productivity through intelligent assistance and automation.
How do I create an account?
To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our homepage. Fill in your details, verify your email address, and you're ready to start using MeemoAI.
Is there a mobile app available?
No, not yet but we plan to be releaasing a mobile app soon.
What are the system requirements?
MeemoAI works on any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and requires a stable internet connection. For mobile devices, iOS 12+ or Android 8+ is recommended.

Features & Usage

What features are included in the free plan?
The free plan includes basic AI assistance, up to 100 queries per month, access to standard templates, and basic automation features. Check our pricing page for more details.
Can I integrate MeemoAI with other tools?
Yes! MeemoAI supports integration with popular tools like Slack, Microsoft Teams, Google Workspace, and more. Visit our integrations page to learn more.
What types of tasks can MeemoAI automate?
MeemoAI can automate email responses, schedule meetings, generate reports, analyze data, create content, and handle various repetitive tasks. Our AI can be trained for custom automation workflows specific to your needs.
How secure is my data with MeemoAI?
We use enterprise-grade encryption and follow strict security protocols. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We are GDPR compliant and never share your information with third parties.

Billing & Support

How does billing work?
We offer monthly and annual subscription plans. You'll be billed at the start of each billing cycle, and you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time.
How can I get support?
We offer 24/7 support through our help center, email support, and live chat for premium users. You can also join our community forum to get help from other users.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise customers. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with our service, contact our support team within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
Are there any long-term contracts?
No, all our plans are subscription-based with no long-term commitment required. You can cancel at any time and your service will continue until the end of your current billing period.
